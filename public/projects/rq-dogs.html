RQ-Dogs

The best police dog mod for GTA V/FiveM.

About RQ-Dogs

RQ-Dogs is a police dog mod for the popular GTA V launcher FiveM. FiveM allows players to run their own multiplayer GTA V servers, and install mods of their choosing in those servers.

RQ-Dogs was the first police dog mod to truly replicate the experience of being a dog handler in the real world. The dog runs in front of you, restricted by a lead that you can adjust the length of. The dog can pick up a scent from an abandoned vehicle and track a player all over the map from a scent. The dog can be let off the leash and target an offender, running faster than any player character, and tackle and restrain them.

The dog can search for, and pick up the scent of illicit items dropped in the area. It can also sniff cars and players for illicit items, alerting the dog handler if it finds any. The dog can be used as an intimidation tactic, and set to bark and jump at (restricted by a lead), an offender. You can take the dog off the lead and it will heel, walking next to you rather than in front, matching your speed. The dog can sit on command, and jump in and out of your car as you command it to.

The entire resource is highly-configurable by the server owner that installs the script. Every piece of language can be changed, the behaviour of the dog, what items are considered illicit, etc. etc.

I sell RQ-Dogs on FiveM's marketplace, and server owners purchase the resource for use in their own server.

Why

I initially developed RQ-Dogs as a bespoke solution for the server I was playing/working on at the time. I used that server as a beta tester, and seeing the hugely positive feedback from the playerbase, opted to sell the resource.
