Manifest

A Yacht Club guest sign-in kiosk and analysis platform.

About Manifest

Manifest allows Club members to scan their membership card and sign in their guests to the Club.

It's presented on a touch-screen Kiosk at the entrance to the Club. Names of previous guests that individual members have signed in can be used as autofill suggestions when that member goes to sign them in again. There are staff pins that present a list of that day's sign ins on the Kiosk, in the event they are required. Sign ins are otherwise reviewed in the staff portal.

The staff portal allows Club staff to review sign ins for each night, as well as identify guests that are repeatedly being signed in.

To learn more about how Manifest and my other BBYC projects access and update the Clubs membership records, see my project Lighthouse.

Why

The Club is undergoing/underwent a transformation into the digital era and I worked with/for the Club on doing so.

Sign ins were previously being recorded by members in a book at the entrance. This was not only impossible to review, but also a bit of a privacy nightmare with a lot of PII in each entry of the book.

The Club's liquor license instructs it to only serve alcohol to Club members, or registered guests of Club members.

Tech Stack

  • Written in TypeScript using Deno as a runtime.
  • The frontend is built using Tailwind CSS.
  • PostgreSQL is used as a database.
  • I was/am the sole developer working on this for the Club, however I worked with/for the Club Management, Board, and Digital Committee in the design and implementation.
