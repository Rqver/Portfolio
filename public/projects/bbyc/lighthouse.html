Lighthouse

A bridge between accounting/POS/custom software for the Yacht Club.

About Lighthouse

Lighthouse is the bridge between the Club's accounting software, the custom software I've written for the Club, and the Club's new Point-of-Sale software. There's no frontend to the project, hence the pictures of the Club instead.

Lighthouse uses MYOB's API to poll for the membership list every 15 minutes. Several checks are ran on each member to ensure their membership is valid, before being upserted into a PostgreSQL database. Said database is what Mainsheet & Manifest use to source member information.

As a part of the Club's digital overhaul, the bar & restaurant Point-of-Sale software was upgraded, and the Club wanted the ability to record what members purchase what & when. Members can scan their membership card at the bar, the FOH staff are given the member's name, and the purchase is associated with the member. Lighthouse is responsible for upserting and editing the Point-of-Sale software's record of club members, however the implementation of this is unfortunately protected by an NDA.

Why

The Club is undergoing/underwent a transformation into the digital era and I worked with/for the Club on doing so.

Creating Lighthouse allowed me to massively simplify the way my other BBYC projects accessed member information.

Tech Stack

  • Written in TypeScript using Deno as a runtime.
  • PostgreSQL is used as a database.
  • I was/am the sole developer working on this for the Club, however I worked with/for the Club Management, Board, and Digital Committee in the design and implementation.
