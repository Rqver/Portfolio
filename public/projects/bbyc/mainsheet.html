Mainsheet

An all-in-one Yacht Club staff & members portal.

About Mainsheet

Mainsheet is split into two parts; A members portal, and a staff portal.

Shared across both portals is a bespoke restaurant booking system for the Club. Club members can make bookings for the restaurant inside the members portal, and staff can manage, review, and create bookings themselves in the staff portal. The members portal is aware of the signed-in members name, email address & phone number, so bookings are fast and easy for members. Staff can review, modify, and assign tables to bookings in the staff portal. Specially restricted staff accounts provide access to a touch-friendly very simple view of only that nights restaurant bookings, designed for use by FOH staff.

Members sign in to the members portal using their unique membership number. As well as restaurant bookings, the portal provides access to a directory of Club members, their phone numbers & boat names, as well as a directory of Club documents that are shared with members.

The staff portal features a global member search, guest-sign in history and analysis, and various other administrative functions of the Club.

To learn more about how Mainsheet and my other BBYC projects access and update the Clubs membership records, see my project Lighthouse.

Why

The Club is undergoing/underwent a transformation into the digital era and I worked with/for the Club on doing so.

Restaurant bookings were previously being recorded in a physical book, and you had to call the club during opening hours to make a booking. The club members directory was previously being provided in a physical book at the start of each year.

Tech Stack

  • Written in TypeScript using Deno as a runtime.
  • The frontend is built using Tailwind CSS.
  • I was/am the sole developer working on this for the Club, however I worked with/for the Club Management, Board, and Digital Committee in the design and implementation.
